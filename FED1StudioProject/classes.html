Class Subjects

Knife Skills

This course will cover basic knife skills such as mince, dice, and julienne all the way up to more advanced techniques, such as filleting salmon.

All About Eggs

This course will teach students to egg-sel at eggs. We will cover scrambling, frying, poaching, and more.

Roasting

Delve into this delicious course where we show you how to roast a whole chicken and various veggies.

Perfect Pastas

We will learn 3 different pasta shapes from homemade pasta dough, accompanied by made from scratch pesto and sauce tomat.

Grill Mastery

Join us outside as we cook fan favorites over an open flame! Pulled pork, steak, and even vegetables.

Beautiful Breads

Terrified by breadmaking? Have no fear, Meal Master Academy is here! We will be whipping up homemade pizza dough, dinner rolls, and sourdough bread.
