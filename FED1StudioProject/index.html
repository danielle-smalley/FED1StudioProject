About Us

Meal Magic Academy was created to help anyone and everyone learn how to cook. Our mission is to build confidence in the kitchen for our students. Cooking can seem intimidating, but we focus on basics and making food FUN. Our instructors are ready to help you learn and create delicious meals, so roll up your sleeves and let's get cooking!




Why Choose Us

Here at Meal Magic Academy, we have all levels of home cooks in mind--from absolute beginners, to intermediate cooks, all the way up to advanced home chefs. We have courses for everyone! Our instructors believe that no one is a lost cause; they are here to cheer you on every step of the way!

If our stellar staff wasn't enough, we also offer an amazing cooking studio stocked with top-of-the-line equipment and farm-fresh ingredients sourced from our community farmer's market! We only use ingredients you could easily find yourself, and we keep your budget in mind as well.
